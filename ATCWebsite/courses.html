Structure of Training Programs

Our ICT traineeships are designed to provide both theoretical and practical knowledge in Information and Communication Technology. The course structure includes:

  • Core ICT theory and foundation subjects
  • Practical hands-on training sessions
  • Industry-based projects and assessments
  • Mentorship and guidance from industry experts
  • Workplace training in real-world ICT environments

Entry Requirements

Educational Background

To enroll in our Certificate III and IV programs, you will need to meet the following educational criteria:

  • Certificate III: Completion of Year 10 or equivalent
  • Certificate IV: Completion of Year 12 or Certificate III in ICT

Payment Information

Our payment plans are flexible to ensure everyone has access to quality education. Payment options include:

  • Pay per term
  • Monthly installment plans
  • Eligible students may apply for financial aid or government support
